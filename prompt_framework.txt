PROMPT FRAMEWORK – SEO CONTENT GENERATOR

Objective:
Generate SEO-optimized blog content that is informative, engaging, and designed to improve search engine visibility.

Business Information Required:
- Business Name
- Industry
- Services
- Target Audience
- Location
- Primary Keywords

Content Requirements:
- Generate an SEO-friendly title.
- Create a structured blog outline using H1, H2, and H3 headings.
- Write clear, informative, and human-readable content.
- Include naturally placed keywords.
- Suggest internal linking opportunities.
- End with a business-focused conclusion and Call-To-Action.

SEO Requirements:
- Focus on search intent.
- Use keyword-rich headings naturally.
- Optimize for readability.
- Avoid keyword stuffing.

Expected Output:
- SEO Blog Title
- Blog Outline
- Full Blog Content
- Internal Linking Suggestions
- Meta Description
- Call-To-Action
